About Us

Welcome to WalkMeWellMD, the trusted destination for expert foot and ankle care. Led by Dr. Ali Al Hadadd, a highly trained and experienced foot and ankle specialist, we are dedicated to helping you walk, move, and live better. At WalkMeWellMD, our mission is to deliver exceptional medical services tailored to your individual needs. Whether you are seeking treatment for an injury, a chronic condition, or preventive care, Dr. Al Hadadd and his team are here to support your journey to wellness. Through our website, you can easily explore our wide range of services, view our clinic location through Google Maps, and book an appointment with just a few clicks. We believe in making expert care accessible, personalized, and convenient for every patient.
